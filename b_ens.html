B. Entrepreneurship and Start-ups

Venture capital investments in % of GDP 2017–2023

Venture capital availability in has been increasing steadily till 2021/2022, but has declined in the last year because - among other factors - the interest rate reversals. The VC-numbers until Q3 of 2024 show that the performance in 2024 will be comparable or slightly below 2024.

According to the report "Fast-growing start-ups in Switzerland" (SECO, 2017) the venture capital market in was not as mature as the markets in more advanced countries (such as , the ). The VC market has since expanded, but there is still room for improvement.

Total early-stage entrepreneurial activity (TEA)

The share of 18-64 population who are either a nascent entrepreneur or owner-manager of a new business in has been hovering between 8 and 10 percent. This is a low- to mid-table position among innovation peers.
