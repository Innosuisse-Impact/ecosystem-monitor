Sustainability and High-tech Exports

Resource productivity

's economy performs very well in terms of resource and production-based CO2 productivity, becoming more efficient and is among the leaders of its innovation peers.

Resource productivity is measured as the total amout of materials directly used by an economy (domestic material consumption, DMC) in relation to its GDP (GDP per DMC in Euros per kg). It measures how efficiently rescources are being used in the production processes and the progress in decoupling between the use of natural rescources and economic growth. We display the OECD version of the indicator that excludes energy from the materials used (unlike the EIS indicator). performs even better if energy materials are included.
