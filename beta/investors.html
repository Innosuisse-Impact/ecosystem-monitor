Technology Intensity of Investment Portfolios

The European Patent Office (EPO) links its own patent data to the data of dealroom.co on investors and their investments and creates an extensive and detailed comparative analysis of the technology intensity of the investment portfolios in Europe and the US.

The EPO introduces the metric Technology Investor Score (TIS) designed to identify investors specialised in supporting high-tech companies. The TIS measures the percentage of companies with at least one patent within an investor’s portfolio. A high TIS indicates an investor’s engagement with innovation-driven companies.

The analysis reveals "significant funding gaps between Europe and the US for high-TIS private investors, particularly in critical technology sectors with high growth potential. These gaps are also most evident in the later-stage funding rounds essential for scaling up. Instead, we find a funding surplus for public investors."

These gaps also hold true for Switzerland (vis-a-vis the US). But they are less pronounced than in other European countries, because Switzerland ranks at the top in most of the categories compared to other European countries (more mature VC-market, investors with high TIS-Scores, etc.)
