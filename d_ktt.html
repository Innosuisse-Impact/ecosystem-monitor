D. Knowledge and Technology Transfer

PCT patent applications per billion GDP (in PPS)

is well-positioned and its PCT patent applications per billion GDP have grown steadliy with the exceptions of 2019. This holds true as well if we look at patents per capita.

PCT patents are international patents filed through the WIPO-administered Patent Cooperation Treaty. It makes it possible to seek patent protection for an invention simultaneously in a number of countries by filing a single application.

Start-ups from Swiss public research organisations

Universities and other public research organisations have become an important driver of start-up growth in Switzerland. However, recent years have seen a decline both in total start-ups and more pronounced in spin-offs (start-ups with a formal licence of IP).

The swiTT report gives a relativly complete picture for start-ups and spin-offs from ETH and EPFL, other universities and universities of applied sciences are less well represented.
